Palak Dosa

Bored of same old dosas? Try this Palak Dosa recipe to bring about the much-needed change to your palate. Made with spinach leaves blended and added to the dosa batter, this dosa recipe is not just filling for the stomach but also satiates your soul. Served with sambar and coconut chutney, this is a perfect breakfast recipe that will provide a healthy kickstart to your day. Occasions like kitty parties, potlucks and game nights are apt to relish this vegetarian recipe and will surely leave your guests astounded with the taste of this green colored dosa. You can even pack this up for your family and kids in their tiffin. So, don't hold on and prepare this easy recipe for your loved ones this weekend!

prep time

30 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Street food breakfast food

instructions

step 1

Firstly, wash spinach leaves under cold water and keep it aside. Now, take a grinder and add spinach and water into it. Grind the leaves until you get a paste-like consistency. Transfer it in large bowl and add dosa batter in it. Mix it well and make sure that the consistency remains the same.

step 2

Sprinkle salt, red chilli powder and cumin into the batter. Give it a stir and set it aside for some time. Now, place a skillet over medium flame and heat olive oil in it. When the oil is hot enough, using a ladle, transfer the batter onto the skillet and spread it evenly.

step 3

When the dosa starts to cook, you will notice tiny bubbles rising. Flip the dosa and fry it on the other side as well. Once done, transfer it in a plate and sprinkle some coconut flakes over it. Repeat the same steps with the remaining batter and make the dosas. Serve these warm with sambar and coconut chutney to enjoy!

ingredients

2 bunches spinach

2 cup dosa batter

1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder

3 teaspoon virgin olive oil

1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds

salt as required

1/2 cup water
