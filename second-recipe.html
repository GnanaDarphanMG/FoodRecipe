Potato Chips

Baking, frying and microwaving are 3 ways in which you can make potato chips at home. While deep frying the chips make them high in calories, but despite it, some people can't resist having it. For those who are conscious about their health, they should use the other two methods.

prep time

50 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Street food breakfast food

instructions

step 1

Wash the potatoes properly and peel them using a peeler knife. Then, thinly slice them.

step 2

Add the salt in a large bowl of water and add the potato slices to it. Keep aside for around 30 minutes. Then, drain the water and rinse the slices again and dry them.

step 3

Now, heat oil in a skillet over a medium flame and drop the potato slices to it to deep fry them.

step 4

Once done, remove and drain the excess oil with clean paper towels and serve.

ingredients

4 potatoes

4 cups oil for deep frying

3 tablespoons salt
